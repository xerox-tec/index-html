About Us

Oyoko Methodist SHS is a co-educational institution located in Koforidua-Oyoko , Ghana. Our school is committed to providing quality education to our students, helping them to become responsible and productive citizens.

Achievements

Our students have achieved excellent results in various exams and competitions, including:

  • 100% pass rate in the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE)
  • Winner of the National Science and Maths Quiz
  • Recipient of the President's Award for Academic Excellence
  • Our students have won several awards for their community service projects
